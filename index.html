About us

Floriana Fortesque - it's 100% natural and high-quality ice cream. History of our brand goes back to 1994 in picturesque Kyiv city. From that time our family have been working in the sphere of production of the real ice cream. All these years we continue to develop and create new recipes of an ice cream.

All the ice cream we make is natural for 100%, as we make it from the very beginning. No bases, stabilizing bases, flavorings or semi-finished products are used for production. Only selected ingredients enter the ice cream. This is why we are confident in the quality of our ice cream.

With our help you can get to know, fall in love and get high from different flavors of natural ice cream, the range of which will constantly change, grow and surprise you.
